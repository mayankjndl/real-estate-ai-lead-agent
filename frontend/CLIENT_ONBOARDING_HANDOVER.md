# Welcome to Your Revenue OS Workspace

Welcome! This guide will walk you through the basics of using your new AI-powered Real Estate Dashboard. The system works in the background to engage leads, qualify them, and organize them so you can focus on closing deals.

## 1. Logging In
- Navigate to your agency's custom login portal.
- Enter your registered email address and password.
- *Security Note:* Your session is secured automatically. If you are inactive for an extended period, you may be asked to log in again to protect your clients' data.

## 2. Dashboard & ROI Analytics
When you log in, you will land on the main **Dashboard**.
- **Overview:** Here you can see a high-level view of your current performance.
- **ROI Tracking:** View statistics on your Return on Investment, giving you a clear picture of how much value the AI is generating.
- **Funnel Tracking:** This section shows how many leads are entering the system and progressing toward a closed deal.

## 3. The Lead Inbox
Navigate to the **Leads** tab using the side menu.
- **Real-Time Data:** This table displays all leads currently interacting with the AI via WhatsApp.
- **Intelligence:** You can see each lead's intent, their budget range, and how "hot" or "cold" they are based on their engagement.
- **Mobile Friendly:** If you are viewing this on your phone, you can swipe horizontally on the table to view all the data columns.

## 4. The CRM Kanban Board
Navigate to the **CRM** tab. This is your interactive workspace for tracking deal progress.
- **Stages:** Leads are categorized into stages like "New", "Contacted", "Appointment Scheduled", and "Closed Won".
- **Drag and Drop:** As you progress a lead through the sales cycle, simply click (or tap and hold on mobile) on a lead card and drag it into the next appropriate stage column. The system will automatically save this update.

## 5. Adjusting Your Settings
Navigate to the **Settings** tab.
- Here you can manage your personal preferences, update your agency profile, and configure notification alerts.
